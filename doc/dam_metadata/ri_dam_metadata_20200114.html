Description:
Abstract: This dataset was originally created for use by the Rhode Island Dept. Of Environmental Management as a general aid in locating dams for inspection under the Division of Compliance &amp; Inspection's Dam Safety Program. The dataset does not include all of the height, width, spillway elevation, impoundment size, construction date &amp; material, etc, found in earlier versions. The most important attribute of this dataset is the hazard class. A high or significant hazard does not imply that a failure is imminent, only that failure would result in loss of life and property. There are known errors in the attributes. Persons desiring detailed information on any of the locations described in this data should contact the Rhode Island Dept. of Environmental Management, Office of Compliance and Inspection http://www.dem.ri.gov/programs/complianceandinspection/
Purpose: Point dataset describing the general location of dams and related structures on rivers or streams within Rhode Island.
Supplemental Information: The dataset does not include all of the height, width, spillway elevation, impoundment size, construction date & material, etc, found in earlier versions. The most important attribute of this dataset is the hazard class. A high or significant hazard does not imply that a failure is imminent, only that failure would result in loss of life and property. There are known errors in the attributes. Persons desiring detailed information on any of the locations described in this data should contact the Rhode Island Dept of Environmental Management, Office of Compliance and Inspection (http://www.dem.ri.gov/programs/benviron/compinsp/dams.htm; last date accessed: 10 September 2014).

Process Step:
Process Description: 
Original source data came from a database maintained by the state dam safety inspector, maintained via field inspections and research. Original dam locations determined by using USGS 1:24,000 Topo Maps. Location of dams further refined with the assistance of recent generations of statewide orthophotography available from RIGIS.
